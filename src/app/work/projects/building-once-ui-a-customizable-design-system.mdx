## Overview

OptiGrade is an AI-driven edutech solution tailored for Nigerian undergraduates. The platform transforms academic outcomes by analyzing student behavior, learning history, and course performance to provide personalized study plans, CGPA predictions, and motivational guidance.

The design system was prototyped entirely in Figma and developed using Next.js + Once UI. The CGPA prediction engine was trained in Google Colab and deployed via Google AI Studio for scalable integration.

## Problem Statement

Many undergraduates struggle with ineffective study habits, time management, and lack of clarity about academic strengths. These challenges lead to:
- Lower grades
- Missed scholarship opportunities
- Burnout and lack of confidence
